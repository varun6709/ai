About Dr. Sai Charan

With 4 years of dedicated experience in physiotherapy and rehabilitation, Dr. Sai Charan specializes in neurological conditions and provides comprehensive care for patients of all ages. Holding an MPT in Neurology, he combines clinical expertise with compassionate care to help patients achieve optimal recovery and improved quality of life.

Dr. Sai Charan is committed to evidence-based treatment approaches and stays updated with the latest advancements in physiotherapy and rehabilitation medicine. His patient-centered approach ensures that each treatment plan is tailored to individual needs and recovery goals.
